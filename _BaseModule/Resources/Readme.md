# The WriteEverywhere Resource Folder.

Here you have following things:
- atlases
    - Here are the Atlas Folder which contains your used images
- fonts
    - Here are the fonts used by your Module
- layouts
    - Here are the layouts or defaults which can be either imported by the enduser or be automaticly applied by WE.
- objMeshes
    - Here are the .obj 3D Modules used by your Module
